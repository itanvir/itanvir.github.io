About

Hello! I'm Tanvir Islam.

I am a machine learning scientist specializing in both the theoretical and applied aspects of machine learning. My work spans researching novel ML algorithms, as well as designing and building robust ML systems that solve complex, real-world problems.

Currently, I lead the development of data-driven systems to defend against emerging cybersecurity threats at Okta. My work involves developing innovative solutions that leverage machine learning to enhance security measures, mitigate risks, and prevent fraud.

Prior to joining Okta, I held prestigious research scientist positions at NASA JPL, Caltech, and NOAA, where I focused on data-driven research and developed advanced modeling techniques to tackle complex scientific problems. I hold a PhD in Engineering (Machine Learning and Sensing) from the University of Bristol, UK.

My extensive work has resulted in numerous publications and patents in machine learning research and applications.
